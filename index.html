Our Craft

The Art of Patience & Precision

At Artisan Patisserie / Bakery, we believe that exceptional baking cannot be rushed. It requires an absolute dedication to time, temperature, and texture. Every morning, our bakers begin long before dawn, nursing wild starters and laminating organic butter into countless delicate layers.

We source only the finest heritage grains, cold-pressed dairy, and hand-harvested sea salt. Combined with seasonal fruits plucked at the peak of sweetness, our creations carry a signature character—indulgent yet refined, simple yet complex.

Curiosities

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common inquiries regarding orders, customization, and delivery options.

We ask that all bespoke cake orders be finalized at least 2 to 3 weeks prior to your event date. This ensures we have ample time to source custom ingredients, coordinate structural designs, and dedicate proper care to your creation. For larger catering bookings, we recommend securing your date 1 to 2 months in advance.

While we are primarily a traditional wheat-flour patisserie, we do offer select items crafted without gluten or animal products in our seasonal menus. However, please note that all our pastries are prepared in a shared kitchen space with wheat, dairy, eggs, and nuts. We cannot guarantee complete absence of cross-contact.

Our weekly box is billed automatically and delivered each Saturday morning to your doorstep. You can pause, skip a week, or cancel your subscription at any time directly through your email dashboard or by reaching out to us before Wednesday noon.

We are delighted to design personalized flavor profiles for our bespoke celebration cakes! Our pastry chef will consult with you to pair base sponges, custom fruit curds, and botanical-infused buttercreams to create a signature flavor that reflects your preferences.

We offer hand-couriered delivery within a 15-mile radius of our main boutique boutique kitchen. The delivery fee is calculated at checkout based on mileage. For delicate items like cakes, we utilize temperature-controlled vehicles to ensure your bakes arrive in pristine condition.
